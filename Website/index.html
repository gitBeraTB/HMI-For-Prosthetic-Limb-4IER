Capabilities

Key Features

01

Smart AI on Device

The system uses a built-in artificial intelligence model to instantly recognize muscle movements right on the device.

02

Clear Signal Quality

It automatically cleans up electrical noise and interference, ensuring that only true muscle signals are captured.

03

Detailed Movement Analysis

By continuously analyzing multiple patterns in the muscle signals, it accurately detects exactly what movement you intend to make.

04

Instant Wireless Connection

A high-speed wireless connection ensures the robotic arm reacts to your thoughts tolerable delay.

05

Live Monitoring

Comes with an easy-to-use computer program that lets users and doctors see their muscle activity in real-time.

06

Easily Trainable

The system can be trained easily by turning collecting mode ON
